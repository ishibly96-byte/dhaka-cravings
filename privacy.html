Draft Policy

Privacy

What We Collect

We collect order items, delivery address, phone/contact details, approximate geolocation when permitted, and payment confirmation metadata.

Payments

Card details are handled by Stripe-hosted Checkout. PayPal payments are handled by PayPal. Dhaka Cravings does not store raw card numbers.

Firebase & Orders

Firebase may store authenticated order history, order statuses, support notes, and admin operational data under protected rules.

Retention

Order records are kept only as long as needed for fulfillment, support, compliance, and fraud prevention.
